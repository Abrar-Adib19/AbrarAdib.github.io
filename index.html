Projects

Below are some of the projects that I've completed throughout my academic pursuit.

4 to 2 bit Binary Encoder using Cadence Virtuoso

The schematic and corresponding Layout was designed in Cadence Virtuoso. Additionally, Design Rule Check (DRC) and Layout Versus Schematic (LVS) verification was performed to ensure the design's accuracy.

Design of a Lux Meter to Measure The Intensity of Light

Determined the level of illumination in different lighting conditions indoors and outdoors.

Design and Implementation of a Water Level Indicator

Designed and implemented a device which can detect multiple levels of water in a container. We used LEDs to measure the height of water and the device showed warning when the container was full.


Design and Simulation of a 2 input NAND Gate in Cadence Virtuoso

The schematic and corresponding Layout was designed in Cadence Virtuoso. Additionally, Design Rule Check (DRC) and Layout Versus Schematic (LVS) verification was performed to ensure the design's accuracy.

Design and Optimization of CH3NH3SnBr3/CuInSe2 Monolithic Tandem Solar Cell with Enhanced Efficiency using SCAPS-1D

Designed a novel structure for tandem solar cell by testing the combination of various layers. Optimized the selected layers to enhance the performance metrics of the solar cell.
